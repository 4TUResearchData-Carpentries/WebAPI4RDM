Display setup: 
•	Large projection screen or multiple screens so all participants can clearly see the live coding.
•	Dual monitors (if possible) for the instructor to manage notes and live coding separately.

Internet and power
•	Stable, high-speed internet connection for cloning repositories, pushing/pulling from GitHub, and accessing documentation.
•	Power outlets distributed across the room for participants' laptops.

Room arrangement
•	U-shape or classroom style seating so participants can see both the screen and interact with their laptops.
•	Space for helpers to move around and assist attendees.

Audio Setup
•	Wireless microphone/headset if the room is large.
•	Good room acoustics to ensure clear audio if you're explaining while typing.

Whiteboard 
•	Whiteboard and markers for illustrating concepts
